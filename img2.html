Futala Lake is a popular and iconic man-made lake located in the heart of Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. It features a beautiful illuminated musical fountain, a jogging track around its perimeter, and is a favored recreational spot for both locals and tourists. Boating is available, and the lake is known for its picturesque beauty and tranquil atmosphere.
