Telankhedi Garden

Loaction:https://maps.app.goo.gl/f7U4i9cX2cgEAVCY6

Telankhedi Garden is a serene urban park located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. It features lush greenery, a picturesque lake, and is a popular spot for picnics, jogging, and pedal boating. The garden provides a peaceful escape in the heart of the city.

Japanese Garden

Nagpur has a Japanese Rose Garden, known for its diverse and beautiful rose varieties. Located in the Civil Lines area, it's a peaceful place for a leisurely stroll and to enjoy the vibrant colors and fragrances of the roses.
