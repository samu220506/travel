Sitabuldi Fort

Loaction:https://maps.app.goo.gl/FDn2uFpMsbB87qm89

Sitabuldi Fort, located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, played a crucial role in the 1857 First War of Indian Independence. Although much of the original fort has been destroyed, a memorial pillar stands in its place. The fort offers panoramic views of Nagpur and is a historically significant site within the city.

Nagardhan Fort

Loaction:https://maps.app.goo.gl/cvUnKeYwvKorZQPt7

Nagardhan Fort, also known as Nandigan Fort, is a historic fort located near Nagardhan village, about 16 kilometers from Nagpur. It has historical significance and features notable architectural elements, including fortification walls and gates. While parts of the fort have deteriorated, it remains a site of historical interest and natural beauty.
